Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MenuCheck

What is MenuCheck?

MenuCheck is a comprehensive restaurant management software designed specifically for Nigerian restaurants to streamline operations, manage orders, and boost sales.

How does the free trial work?

We offer a 3-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

How do customers order using QR code?

Customers simply scan the QR code at their table with their phone camera, which opens your digital menu where they can browse and place orders directly.

Can I manage multiple devices?

Yes! MenuCheck works seamlessly across all your devices - phones, tablets, and computers. All data syncs in real-time.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation required! MenuCheck works entirely in your web browser. Just log in and start managing your restaurant.

Is it secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption and security protocols to protect your data and your customers' information.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. There are no contracts or hidden fees.

Do you support Nigerian payment methods?

Yes! We integrate seamlessly with popular Nigerian payment gateways including Flutterwave, Paystack, and bank transfers.

Can I customize my menu design?

Yes, you can customize colors, fonts, and layout to match your restaurant's branding.

What if I need help?

Our Nigerian-based support team is available via WhatsApp and email to assist you anytime.
